Frequently Asked Questions

Are your apps free to use?

Yes, all our apps are completely free with no hidden costs or in-app purchases. We believe in making Islamic knowledge accessible to everyone.

Do you collect personal data?

We prioritize your privacy. Our apps store data locally on your device and we don't collect personal information without your consent. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Can I request a feature?

Absolutely! We welcome all suggestions. Use the contact form above or reach out on our community forum. We regularly review user feedback for future updates.

How often is the content updated?

We regularly update our content and apps to ensure accuracy and add new features. Updates are typically released monthly, with major updates announced on our website and social media.

Can I use Deenify apps offline?

Yes, most of our apps are designed to work offline. You can download content for offline use in many of our applications, perfect for when you're traveling or have limited internet access.

Do you have plans to develop more apps?

Yes, we are constantly working on new apps and features. Stay tuned by following our updates on GitHub or subscribing to our newsletter for announcements about upcoming releases.

Is the content verified by Islamic scholars?

Yes, all our Islamic content is reviewed by qualified scholars to ensure accuracy and authenticity. We take great care to present information that aligns with mainstream Islamic teachings.

How can I support Deenify?

You can support us by sharing our apps with others, contributing to our open-source projects on GitHub, or providing feedback to help us improve. We also welcome donations to help cover operational costs.
